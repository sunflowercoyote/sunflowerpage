Who is Coyote Sunflower?

A visual artist rooted in the raw, untamed spirit of the Arizona desert, Coyote Sunflower's work is a testament to the forbidden, the feral, and the transcendent.
Each line and texture breathes life into forms that defy restraint: figures that rise like storm-carved mesas, skin shimmering with the heat of ritual, limbs entwined like ancient desert roots.
