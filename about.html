Who is Coyote Sunflower?

Coyote is a visual artist based in New England. His work is a celebration of the divine and the mundane human form; exploring themes of desire, identity, and transformation.

Drawing inspiration from mythology, folklore, and the natural world, Coyote's art invites viewers to explore the liminal spaces between the physical and the metaphysical. Through a combination of traditional and digital media, they create vivid, immersive landscapes that blur the boundaries between reality and fantasy.

A conjurer of raw, unfiltered, primal humanity, Coyote delves into the essence of existence, manifesting bodies unbound by shame and entwined in the dance of shadow and light; His art is a testament to the forbidden, the carnal, and the ethereal.

Each stroke of the brush or line on the canvas breathes life into forms that defy convention: figures that twist and merge like ancient deities, skin glowing with the heat of desire, and eyes that pierce through the veil of metaphysical restraint.
